About

I am a Research Economist at the Bureau of Economic Analysis (BEA). In 2023, I received my PhD in Economics from Boston College. My research combines microdata analysis and structural modeling to study labor market dynamics both in the long run and over the business cycle.

Research and Teaching Fields: Macroeconomics, Labor Economics
Research Interests: Macro Labor, Firm Dynamics, Worker Flows, Structural Change
